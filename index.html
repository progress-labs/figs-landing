Our values

At FIGS, we celebrate healthcare professionals for their unwavering commitment and the incredible impact they make every single day. Our mission is to celebrate, empower, and serve those who serve others through innovative products and advocacy that truly makes a difference. With the support of our incredible team and the strength of our community of Awesome Humans, we're here to make sure you feel recognized, valued, and ready to tackle every challenge that comes your way.

Everything we do is grounded in the following core values:

Make an Impact

Whether it's designing innovative products, launching bold campaigns, or championing advocacy, our focus is always on creating meaningful change for our community.

If It Was Easy, Everyone Would Do It

We break barriers and face challenges head-on—always driving progress for our Awesome Humans.

Have a Bias Toward Action

We move fast and with purpose to support and empower healthcare professionals, stepping up to meet your needs when it matters most.

Care More Than Anyone Else

We lead with empathy and dedication to make sure you feel seen, supported, and valued.

Ubuntu – I Am Because You Are

We believe in the power of community—knowing our greatest strength comes from lifting each other up and building deep connections.

Create the World You Want to Live In

We are shaping a future that puts healthcare professionals' wellbeing and growth front and center.

How You Do Anything Is How You Do Everything

We set the bar high, infusing excellence into everything we create and do for our community.

Letter from Our Founders

In 2024, we were reminded why FIGS exists—to celebrate and uplift our Awesome Humans as you take on challenges most of us can't even imagine. Your resilience and dedication inspires us to innovate, ensuring every shift is met with the quality, comfort, and performance you deserve. We have doubled down on advocacy, driving meaningful change in the policies that impact your lives.

This year, we amplified the voices of healthcare professionals on Capitol Hill, donated a record number of FIGS through Threads for Threads, and gave a spotlight to Team USA's medical team at the 2024 Olympic and Paralympic Games. Your stories drive us to push boundaries, design with purpose, advocate boldly, and create a future where you're truly valued. Thank you for inspiring us to build the world we all want to live in.
